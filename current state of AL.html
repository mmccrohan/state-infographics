The Dispatcher Crisis

Chronically understaffed centers, fueled by low pay and high stress, lead to burnout and increase the risk of operational errors. Outdated tools amplify cognitive load on every call.

The Language & Data Barrier

Currently, a non-English call requires a slow, costly connection to a third-party interpreter, adding minutes of delay. AI provides instant two-way translation and live transcription, spotting keywords like "fire" or "not breathing" to give dispatchers immediate, critical insight.

Infrastructure Fragility

Frequent natural disasters overwhelm isolated 911 centers. The aging infrastructure lacks the resilience and interoperability to share call loads or data between jurisdictions during a crisis.
