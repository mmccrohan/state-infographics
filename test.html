Critical Challenges on the Front Lines

GIS data gaps and staffing shortages directly impact emergency outcomes, creating disparities in response times and putting immense strain on dispatchers.

The Rural-Urban Divide in Response

National data shows vast geography and GIS data gaps create a significant disparity in how quickly help can arrive. Average EMS response times in rural areas are often more than double those in urban centers.

The Weight of Staffing Shortages

A statewide shortage of public safety telecommunicators puts immense pressure on existing staff, increasing the risk of burnout and errors.

Dispatcher Overload & Burnout

High vacancy rates and manual data entry increase stress, cognitive load, and the risk of errors.

A Unified, Automated Platform

Integrate data into a single screen and automate non-emergency call handling to reduce manual tasks and information overload.

Improved Efficiency & Staff Wellness

Decrease call handling times, support dispatcher focus and well-being, and reduce costly staff turnover.

Language Barriers & Missed Details

Using third-party translation services causes delays, while manual listening risks missing critical keywords in a chaotic call.

Live Translation & Transcription

Serve all communities faster regardless of language, and ensure critical details are never missed, leading to better resource allocation.
